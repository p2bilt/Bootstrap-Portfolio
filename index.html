About Me

I’m a Visual Designer specializing in both full-stack UI creation and more traditional graphic design. To get a sense of my work, take a look at my portfolio or download my résumé — you’ll find an idea-driven creative with the precise experience needed to hit the ground running in a fast-paced, visually generative process. My background creating compelling content in a variety of mediums gives me a unique head start in taking your project to the next level of recognition and use-value. I have the proven know-how to combine creative and usability viewpoints to build world-class web applications. I’m quick, learn fast, have a ton of ideas, and get the work out no matter what. If you could use someone with great creative energy and a superb work ethic — please drop me a line.
